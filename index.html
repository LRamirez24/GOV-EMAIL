Spring 2023

News You Can Use, with Updates & Information from OIS

US Tax Court Office of Information Systems (OIS) is working to transform the court’s Information Technology infrastructure and update the organization’s systems. The objective has many agendas; primarily to improve storage for data and records, to increase speed of performance, to standardize systems to work better within the agency and with other government departments, and to reinforce and supplement our cybersecurity defenses.

Our agenda is very much needed to keep the agency running at the highest levels of efficiency and performance but it is also responsive to President Biden’s agenda to modernize and protect the US government’s IT systems. We have laid out a response in accordance with the US Tax Court’s mission to provide expeditious resolutions and OIS’ vision to provide the court with a superior digital information environment that is continually evolving while maintaining stability, security, and staying within budget.

Many of these upgrades will not be noticed by the majority of the agency, and that is by design. We are upgrading and fortifying while being mindful of the daily work of USTC as well as engagement and interaction with civilians. We plan to work behind the scenes to provide little disruption to the court’s everyday operations.

OIS improvements to USTC will make processes and procedures easier for the organization to run smoother. We will also be making updates to both the programs and the devices that the judges use to improve routine functions. Our plan is to start with updates to the overall operations and overarching network and security components. These updates will support trial sessions and preparations, and resolve the IT issues encountered by the judges and court staff.

OIS will also be better defining the security operations of the court in order to enhance safety and further safeguard the court’s sensitive information. This encapsulates the OIS’s DevOps (development and operations) efforts in updating and expanding programs in order to make the court faster, safer, and more effective.

Get to know your team of OIS IT professionals.

The OIS is comprised of experienced and exceptionally-trained individuals whose efforts and work collectively drives our mission. Individually they provide support to the US Tax Court judges, lawyers, paralegals, and court officials for optimal functionality and customer experience.
